Apple Watch Series 9

Smarter. Brighter. Mightier.

Design

Beautiful and durable

Apple Watch Series 9 features a stunning aluminum or stainless steel case with crack-resistant front crystal. Available in 41mm and 45mm sizes. Choose from a wide range of band options including Sport Band, Sport Loop, Braided Solo Loop, and more. Available in Midnight, Starlight, Silver, (PRODUCT)RED, and Pink.

Display

Always-On Retina display

Brilliant Always-On Retina display with up to 2000 nits brightness — twice as bright as Series 8. LTPO OLED technology for power efficiency. Force Touch and Digital Crown with haptic feedback for intuitive navigation. Display stays visible in direct sunlight.

Performance

S9 SiP — blazing fast performance

Apple S9 System in Package with custom CPU and GPU delivers up to 2x faster performance. 4-core Neural Engine processes machine learning tasks on device. Faster Siri processing, smoother animations, and enhanced app performance.

Health

Advanced health sensors

Blood oxygen sensor, ECG app, heart rate monitoring with high and low heart rate notifications. Sleep tracking, temperature sensing, and cycle tracking. Fall Detection, Crash Detection, and Emergency SOS. Mindfulness app for meditation and reflection.

Fitness

Your ultimate fitness companion

Track over 80 workout types with advanced metrics. GPS + Cellular lets you leave your phone behind. Water resistant to 50 meters for swimming. Activity rings motivate you to move, exercise, and stand throughout the day. Precision dual-frequency GPS for accurate distance and pace.

Battery

All-day battery life

Up to 18 hours of all-day battery life with typical use. Up to 36 hours in Low Power Mode. Fast charging gets you to 80% in about 45 minutes. Optimized charging learns your routine to extend battery lifespan.

Connectivity

GPS + Cellular capability

GPS + Cellular model lets you make calls, send texts, stream music, and get directions without your iPhone. Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 5.3 for seamless connectivity. Apple Pay for contactless payments. Family Setup lets family members without iPhone use Apple Watch.

Software

watchOS — powerful features

watchOS brings thousands of apps to your wrist. Customizable watch faces, complications, and widgets. Seamless integration with iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Regular updates add new features and health capabilities. Raise to Speak for instant Siri access.

Why choose

Health and fitness on your wrist

Apple Watch Series 9 combines advanced health monitoring, comprehensive fitness tracking, smart connectivity, and safety features — all in a beautifully designed device that helps you live a healthier, more connected life.

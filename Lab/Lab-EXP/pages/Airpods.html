AirPods Pro (2nd generation)

Rebuilt from the sound up.

Design

Iconic and comfortable

Redesigned AirPods Pro feature a shorter stem and a customizable fit with four pairs of silicone ear tips (XS, S, M, L). The premium white finish with precision-machined details creates a seamless, elegant look. IPX4 water and sweat resistance for workouts and all-weather use.

Audio

Adaptive Audio & Transparency

Active Noise Cancellation blocks outside noise for immersive sound. Adaptive Audio dynamically adjusts noise control based on your environment. Transparency mode lets you hear the world around you. Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking for theater-like sound.

Performance

H2 chip — next-level intelligence

Apple's H2 chip delivers smarter noise cancellation, more immersive sound, and enhanced battery efficiency. Computational audio adapts in real-time for crystal-clear calls and music. Low-latency audio for gaming and video.

Controls

Touch control on stem

Control music and calls with simple swipes and presses on the stem. Volume control with swipe gestures. Press to play, pause, skip tracks, or answer calls. Voice commands with "Hey Siri" for hands-free control.

Battery

All-day listening with MagSafe

Up to 6 hours of listening time with ANC enabled, up to 30 hours total with the MagSafe Charging Case. Fast charging delivers 1 hour of listening from just 5 minutes of charging. Supports USB-C, MagSafe, Qi wireless, and Apple Watch charger.

Connectivity

Seamless switching & Find My

Automatic switching between iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch. Bluetooth 5.3 for stable connection. Precision Finding helps you locate your AirPods with the Find My app. Built-in speaker in the case for easy location.

Features

Pro features for everyone

Conversation Boost for clearer dialogue. Adaptive EQ tunes music to your ear shape. Vent system for pressure equalization. Skin-detect sensors pause audio when removed from ear.

Compatibility

Works with Apple ecosystem

Compatible with iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, Apple TV, and more. Requires iOS 16, iPadOS 16, macOS Ventura, watchOS 9, or tvOS 16 or later for full features. Also works with non-Apple devices via Bluetooth.

Why choose

The ultimate listening experience

Industry-leading noise cancellation, adaptive audio technology, spatial sound, and seamless integration with all your Apple devices — all in a compact, comfortable design.

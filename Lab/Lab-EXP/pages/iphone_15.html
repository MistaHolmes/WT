iPhone 15

New camera. New design. Newphoria.

Design

Color-infused glass & aluminum design

The iPhone 15 features a durable aluminum frame with color-infused glass back and Ceramic Shield front. Available in Pink, Yellow, Green, Blue, and Black finishes. Contoured edges for a comfortable grip with precision-machined buttons.

Display

6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display

6.1-inch OLED Super Retina XDR display with True Tone technology. HDR support, True Tone, and excellent brightness for outdoor viewing. Accurate color reproduction ideal for photos and videos.

Performance

A16 Bionic — Advanced performance

Apple's A16 Bionic chip with 6-core CPU and 5-core GPU, plus a 16-core Neural Engine. Delivers fast app launches, smooth gaming, and efficient performance for everyday tasks and demanding applications.

Camera

Advanced camera system

48MP main camera with 2x optical zoom quality, plus 12MP ultra-wide for expansive shots. Photonic Engine for incredible detail and color in low-light photos. Night mode, Portrait mode with advanced bokeh, and Smart HDR 5.

Battery

All-day battery + USB-C

All-day battery life with USB-C fast charging support. Compatible with MagSafe wireless charging and accessories. Power efficiency optimized by A16 Bionic chip and intelligent iOS battery management.

Connectivity

5G, Wi-Fi 6 & more

5G connectivity, Wi-Fi 6 support, Bluetooth 5.3, NFC, Face ID technology, and USB-C port for universal charging and fast data transfer. Enhanced antenna design ensures excellent call quality and network performance.

Storage

Storage options

Available in 128GB, 256GB, and 512GB configurations to meet the needs of casual users, content creators, and everyone in between.

Software

iOS — updates & ecosystem

Comes with the latest iOS version and receives years of software updates. Perfect integration with the Apple ecosystem including iCloud, FaceTime, Continuity, Handoff, AirDrop, and seamless device switching.

Why choose

Made for everyday users and enthusiasts

Combines advanced features with user-friendly design: powerful A16 Bionic performance, impressive camera capabilities, Dynamic Island interface, vibrant colors, and durable build — perfect for daily use and creative work.

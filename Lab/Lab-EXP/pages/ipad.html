iPad Air

Light. Bright. Full of might.

Design

Sleek all-screen design

iPad Air features a stunning 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display with slim bezels and a sleek aluminum unibody design. Available in Space Gray, Starlight, Pink, Purple, and Blue. Touch ID integrated into the top button for secure authentication and Apple Pay.

Display

10.9-inch Liquid Retina display

Beautiful 10.9-inch LED-backlit display with 2360-by-1640 resolution at 264 pixels per inch. Wide color (P3), True Tone technology, and anti-reflective coating for comfortable viewing in any light. Perfect for content creation, entertainment, and productivity.

Performance

M1 chip — desktop-class performance

Apple M1 chip delivers incredible performance with 8-core CPU and 8-core GPU. Up to 60% faster CPU and 2x faster graphics than previous generation. 16-core Neural Engine for advanced machine learning. Desktop-class apps run smoothly.

Camera

12MP cameras front and back

12MP Wide back camera with Smart HDR 3, 4K video recording, and document scanning. 12MP Ultra Wide front camera with Center Stage that automatically keeps you in frame during video calls. Perfect for FaceTime, Zoom, and content creation.

Battery

All-day battery life

Up to 10 hours of surfing the web on Wi‑Fi or watching video. Up to 9 hours of surfing the web using cellular data. USB-C port for fast charging and connecting accessories. Power efficient M1 chip maximizes battery life.

Connectivity

5G, Wi-Fi 6 & USB-C

5G capable for blazing-fast downloads and streaming. Wi-Fi 6 for faster wireless performance. USB-C connector supports up to 10Gbps data transfer. Works with Magic Keyboard, Smart Keyboard Folio, and Apple Pencil (2nd generation).

Storage

Storage options

Available in 64GB and 256GB configurations. Choose the storage that fits your needs for apps, photos, videos, and files. iCloud provides additional cloud storage options.

Software

iPadOS — multitasking reimagined

iPadOS brings desktop-class capabilities with intuitive touch controls. Stage Manager for advanced multitasking, Split View, Slide Over, and app exposé. Works seamlessly with iPhone, Mac, and Apple Watch via Continuity features.

Why choose

Versatile powerhouse for everyone

Whether you're creating art with Apple Pencil, editing 4K video, playing graphics-intensive games, or staying productive — iPad Air delivers pro-level performance in a portable, lightweight design.
